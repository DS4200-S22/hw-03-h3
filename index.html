HTML+CSS & Marks+Channels

The data type used for this chart are 'items' and 'attributes'. The 'items' are the individual data point. Each data point has two categorical attributes(defines the axes and colors) and one quantitative attribute(defines the height of each bar). The marks used for this chart are 'lines', and they are used as 'items' that contribute to the height of the bars. The channels used for this graph are 'position' and 'color'.
